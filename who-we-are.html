Who We Are

We are a community of Secular Discalced Carmelites (OCDS) who seek deeper union with God through prayer, community, and apostolate in the spirit of the Carmelite saints.

Our Mission

To live the Gospel under the guidance of the Church, nourished by Carmelite spirituality, and to bring Christ to the world through prayer and witness.

What Is the OCDS?

OCDS members are lay men and women (and diocesan clergy) who are called to live Carmelite spirituality in the midst of their daily life.
