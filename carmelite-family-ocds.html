Our Carmelite Family (OCDS)

The Secular Discalced Carmelites are a branch of the Discalced Carmelite Order, sharing the same spirituality and charism while living in the world.

Roots & Charism

Inspired by St. Teresa of Jesus and St. John of the Cross, Carmel emphasizes contemplative prayer, community, and apostolate.

Relationship with the Order

OCDS communities live in communion with the Discalced Carmelite friars and nuns, according to the Constitutions and Statutes.
