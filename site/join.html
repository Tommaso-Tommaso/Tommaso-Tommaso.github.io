What to Expect

  • Warm welcome and brief introductions for first-time visitors
  • Communal prayer and a short formation talk
  • Time for questions and conversation
  • Optional carpool to Columbia on 2nd Sundays for Formation Study

Who Is Welcome

Adults seeking a deeper life of prayer and an introduction to the Carmelite tradition are welcome to visit. There is no cost to attend as a guest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register before coming?

No registration is required. If you’d like, send us a quick note so we can look out for you.

What should I bring?

Just yourself. If you have a Bible or Liturgy of the Hours, feel free to bring it, but it’s not required.

Is there a dress code?

Come as you are. Modest, comfortable clothing is appropriate.

Is there a cost?

No. Voluntary contributions are sometimes collected to cover materials and space, but no one is turned away.

Next Steps

If you feel drawn to continue, we’ll introduce you to our formation process and help you discern at your own pace.
